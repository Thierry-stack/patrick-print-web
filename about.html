About Patrick print

Your premier partner for professional printing services in the heart of Rwanda. Discover our story and commitment to excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by printing industry veterans, Patrick print began as a small shop with two employees and a single printing press. Today, we operate from a modern 1,000 square meter facility in central Kigali with a team of 25 dedicated professionals.

Our growth has been driven by our commitment to quality, customer service, and continuous investment in the latest printing technologies. We're proud to serve businesses of all sizes across Rwanda, from startups to multinational corporations.

What sets us apart is our local expertise combined with international quality standards. We understand the Rwandan market and help our clients communicate effectively with their audiences.

Our Mission & Values

Mission

To empower Rwandan businesses with high-quality printing solutions that enhance their brand image and communication.

Vision

To be Rwanda's most trusted printing partner, recognized for quality, innovation, and customer service.

Values

Quality, integrity, customer focus, innovation, and community impact guide everything we do.
